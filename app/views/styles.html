Make Ofgem services look and feel consistent with the Ofgem Design System.

If you are using the GOV.UK Prototype Kit or have GOV.UK Frontend included in your build, the coded examples in the Ofgem Design System will not need any additional styling.

If you need to apply styles manually, you should still follow existing Ofgem and GOV.UK conventions. For example, do not assign new meanings to colours, do not change the style of buttons or adjust the thickness of borders on form inputs.
